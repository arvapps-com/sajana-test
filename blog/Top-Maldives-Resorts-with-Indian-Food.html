List of Top Maldives Resorts with Indian Food

The Maldives is a dream destination for many, with its stunning overwater bungalows, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. However, one concern for many Indian travelers is the availability of authentic Indian cuisine. Thankfully, many top Maldives resorts cater specifically to this need, ensuring that you can enjoy delicious Indian meals while soaking in the tropical paradise. In this blog, we'll explore some of the best Maldives resorts where you can savor authentic Indian food.

1. Amaya Kuda Rah Maldives Resort

Amaya Kuda Rah Maldives Resort is a luxurious getaway located in the South Ari Atoll. This resort offers a perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and a touch of home with its authentic Indian cuisine. The resort’s Amaya Food Gallery features a variety of dining options, including a dedicated section for Indian cuisine. From spicy curries to fresh naan bread, the chefs at Amaya Kuda Rah ensure that Indian flavors are well-represented. The resort also offers private dining options, allowing you to enjoy your favorite Indian dishes in the comfort of your villa.

Peculiarities:

  • Private Pool Villas: Each villa comes with a private pool and direct access to the beach or lagoon.
  • Diving and Snorkeling: The resort is close to some of the best diving spots in the Maldives, including the Kuda Rah Thila, a famous dive site.
  • Spa and Wellness: Amaya Spa offers a variety of treatments, including traditional Ayurvedic therapies.

FAQs:

Q: Does Amaya Kuda Rah offer vegetarian Indian dishes?

A: Yes, the resort offers a variety of vegetarian Indian dishes, including Jain options.

Q: Can I request special dietary needs?

A: Yes, the resort’s chefs are happy to accommodate special dietary requests.

2. Adaaran Select Hudhuran Fushi

Adaaran Select Hudhuran Fushi is known for its warm hospitality and excellent service. The resort’s Indian restaurant, Indian Pavilion, offers a diverse menu of North and South Indian dishes, including popular favorites like biryani, tandoori chicken, and dosa. The restaurant also caters to specific dietary needs, including vegetarian and Jain meals. The all-inclusive packages at Hudhuran Fushi ensure that you can enjoy unlimited meals and drinks throughout your stay.

Peculiarities:

  • Surfing Paradise: The resort is a popular destination for surfers, with easy access to some of the best surf breaks in the Maldives.
  • Water Villas: Experience the luxury of staying in a water villa with direct access to the turquoise lagoon.
  • Premium All-Inclusive: Enjoy unlimited food and beverages, including Indian dishes, as part of the all-inclusive package.

FAQs:

Q: Is Indian Pavilion open for all meals?

A: Yes, Indian Pavilion is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Q: Do they offer Jain food?

A: Yes, Jain food is available upon request.

3. Olhuveli Beach & Spa Maldives

Located in the South Malé Atoll, Olhuveli Beach & Spa is part of the Sun Siyam Resorts, known for its excellent hospitality and dining options. The resort’s Four Spices restaurant offers a menu featuring Indian, Thai, and Maldivian cuisine. The Indian section of the menu includes a variety of dishes from different regions of India, prepared with authentic spices and ingredients. The resort also offers special dining experiences, such as beach barbecues and private dinners, where Indian cuisine can be specially prepared.

Peculiarities:

  • Multiple Dining Options: The resort has several restaurants and bars, offering a variety of cuisines, including Indian.
  • Water Activities: Olhuveli offers a range of water sports, including windsurfing, kitesurfing, and diving.
  • Family-Friendly: The resort is very kid-friendly, with a dedicated kids' club and family villas.

FAQs:

Q: Does Olhuveli Beach & Spa offer all-inclusive packages?

A: Yes, the resort offers all-inclusive packages that include meals, drinks, and activities.

Q: Are Indian vegetarian dishes available?

A: Yes, Indian vegetarian dishes are available at the Four Spices restaurant.

4. Hideaway Beach Resort & Spa

Hideaway Beach Resort & Spa, located in the northern Haa Alifu Atoll, is a luxurious retreat that offers privacy and seclusion. The resort’s Matheefaru restaurant includes a dedicated section for Indian cuisine, where guests can enjoy a variety of dishes, from creamy butter chicken to spicy vindaloo. The resort also offers tailor-made dining experiences, where you can request your favorite Indian dishes to be served in your villa or on the beach.

Peculiarities:

  • Exclusive Privacy: The villas are designed to offer maximum privacy, making it an ideal choice for honeymooners.
  • Luxury Spa: The Hideaway Spa offers a range of treatments, including traditional Indian Ayurvedic therapies.
  • Personal Butler Service: Each villa comes with a personal butler who can arrange dining experiences, including Indian cuisine.

FAQs:

Q: Can I request Indian food in my villa?

A: Yes, the resort offers in-villa dining with Indian cuisine available on request.

Q: Does the resort offer halal food?

A: Yes, the resort’s restaurants offer halal food, including Indian dishes.

5. JA Manafaru Maldives

JA Manafaru is a luxurious island retreat located in the northernmost atoll of the Maldives. The resort’s White Orchid restaurant offers a variety of Asian cuisines, including Indian. The Indian menu features both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, with dishes prepared by skilled chefs who understand the nuances of Indian cooking. The resort also offers private dining experiences, including beach dinners and in-villa dining with a focus on Indian cuisine.

Peculiarities:

  • Secluded Location: JA Manafaru offers ultimate seclusion, making it a perfect choice for those looking to escape the crowds.
  • Signature Dining: Enjoy signature dining experiences, including a romantic beach dinner with Indian dishes.
  • Wine Cellar: The resort’s wine cellar offers an extensive selection of wines to complement your meals.

FAQs:

Q: Is there a vegetarian Indian menu available?

A: Yes, the Indian menu at White Orchid includes a range of vegetarian options.

Q: Can I book a private beach dinner with Indian food?

A: Yes, the resort offers private beach dinners with Indian cuisine.

6. Kuramathi Maldives

Kuramathi Maldives is one of the largest resorts in the Maldives, offering a wide range of dining options, including Indian cuisine at the Tandoor Mahal restaurant. The restaurant is known for its flavorful tandoori dishes, biryanis, and a variety of vegetarian options. The resort’s diverse dining experiences also include private beach dinners and romantic meals under the stars, where you can enjoy Indian dishes specially prepared for you.

Peculiarities:

  • Extensive Dining Options: Kuramathi offers multiple dining options, with over ten restaurants and bars to choose from.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices: The resort is committed to sustainability, with eco-friendly practices in place.
  • Family-Friendly: Kuramathi is a great option for families, with a kids’ club and family villas available.

FAQs:

Q: Does Tandoor Mahal offer both North and South Indian dishes?

A: Yes, the restaurant offers a mix of North and South Indian dishes.

Q: Are there Indian-themed nights at the resort?

A: Yes, the resort occasionally hosts Indian-themed nights with a special menu.

7. Sun Island Resort & Spa

Sun Island Resort & Spa is located in the South Ari Atoll and is one of the largest resorts in the Maldives. The resort’s Maaniyaa restaurant offers a dedicated Indian cuisine section, featuring a variety of dishes from across India. The chefs use traditional recipes and fresh ingredients to create authentic flavors that will remind you of home. The resort also offers special dining experiences, including private dinners on the beach with a customized Indian menu.

Peculiarities:

  • Wide Range of Activities: Sun Island offers a variety of activities, including diving, fishing, and water sports.
  • Affordable Luxury: The resort offers luxury at an affordable price, making it accessible to a wide range of travelers.
  • Beautiful Beaches: The resort is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the Maldives.

FAQs:

Q: Does Maaniyaa restaurant offer Jain food?

A: Yes, Jain food is available upon request.

Q: Are there any Indian chefs at the resort?

A: Yes, the resort has Indian chefs who specialize in preparing authentic Indian dishes.

8. Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort & Spa

Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort & Spa, located on Furanafushi Island, is a luxurious resort that offers a variety of dining options, including Indian cuisine at its Feast restaurant. The Indian section of the buffet features a range of dishes, from spicy curries to freshly baked naan. The resort also offers private dining experiences, where you can enjoy Indian food in a romantic setting, such as on the beach or in your villa.

Peculiarities:

  • Proximity to Malé: The resort is just a 15-minute speedboat ride from Malé, making it easily accessible.
  • Luxury Spa: The Shine Spa offers a variety of treatments, including Ayurvedic therapies.
  • Family-Friendly: Sheraton Maldives is great for families, with a kids’ club and family villas available.

FAQs:

Q: Is there a separate Indian menu available at Feast?

A: Yes, the Indian section of the buffet offers a variety of dishes, and a separate menu can be arranged upon request.

Q: Can I request Indian food for room service?

A: Yes, Indian food can be ordered through room service.

These top Maldives resorts ensure that Indian travelers can enjoy their vacation without compromising on their dietary preferences. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit the Maldives?

The best time to visit the Maldives is from November to April when the weather is pleasant and dry. This period is ideal for beach activities and water sports.

What activities can I do at the beaches?

Beaches in the Maldives offer a range of activities including snorkeling, diving, kayaking, paddleboarding, and beach volleyball. Many resorts also provide organized excursions and water sports.

Can I find budget accommodation in the Maldives?

Yes, there are budget accommodation options available in the Maldives, particularly on local islands. These options offer a more affordable way to experience the beauty of the Maldives without the high costs of luxury resorts.
