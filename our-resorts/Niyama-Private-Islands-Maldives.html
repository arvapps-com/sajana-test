Niyama Private Islands Maldives: A Dual-Island Playground of Luxury

Niyama Private Islands Maldives offers a unique and vibrant luxury experience, with two islands—Play and Chill—catering to different tastes and moods. Located in the Dhaalu Atoll, this resort is perfect for both families and couples, offering a blend of adventure and relaxation. With its chic villas, innovative dining, and a wide range of activities, Niyama Private Islands Maldives is a true playground of luxury.

Luxurious Accommodations

Niyama Private Islands features a variety of stylish accommodations spread across its two islands. Play is home to family-friendly beachfront villas, each with direct access to the pristine beach and spacious living areas. Chill offers overwater villas perfect for couples, with private pools, sun decks, and breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Each villa is designed to provide a seamless blend of indoor and outdoor living, with modern amenities and luxurious touches that ensure comfort and relaxation.

Gourmet Dining

The resort offers a diverse culinary journey with multiple dining venues, each providing a unique experience. Epicure serves international cuisine in a casual, beachfront setting, while Tribal offers an authentic African-inspired dining experience with live cooking stations. For a truly unforgettable meal, guests can dine at Edge, an overwater restaurant accessible only by boat, or at Subsix, an underwater restaurant offering stunning views of marine life. Niyama’s dining options are designed to delight and surprise, with creative menus and unforgettable settings.

Wellness and Recreation

Niyama Private Islands is a haven for both relaxation and adventure. The Drift Spa offers a range of treatments that combine modern wellness techniques with traditional Maldivian therapies. The spa also features a yoga pavilion, steam rooms, and a relaxation area with stunning ocean views. For those seeking adventure, the resort offers a variety of activities, including diving, snorkelling, surfing, and water sports. The resort’s Explorers kids' club ensures that younger guests are entertained with fun and educational activities throughout their stay.

Tailored Experiences

Niyama Private Islands is dedicated to creating personalised experiences for every guest. Whether it’s a private dinner on the beach, a customised wellness retreat, or a family adventure exploring the surrounding atolls, the resort’s team is committed to making each moment special. The dual-island concept allows guests to choose between relaxation and adventure, ensuring that every stay is tailored to their preferences.

Why Choose Niyama Private Islands Maldives?

In summary, Niyama Private Islands Maldives offers a vibrant and luxurious retreat where adventure meets relaxation. With its chic accommodations, diverse dining options, and a wide range of activities, this dual-island resort provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable holiday in the Maldives.
