A Timeless Sanctuary of Unspoiled Beauty

Vakkaru Maldives is a luxurious island resort located in the Baa Atoll, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, offering an idyllic retreat surrounded by unspoiled natural beauty. Known for its elegant villas, exceptional dining, and personalised service, Vakkaru Maldives is the perfect destination for couples, families, and anyone seeking a serene and romantic escape in the Maldives.

Luxurious Accommodations

The resort offers a range of beachfront and overwater villas, each designed to provide the utmost in comfort and privacy. The villas are elegantly appointed with natural materials, spacious living areas, and private terraces that offer stunning views of the lagoon or ocean. For those seeking an even more luxurious experience, the resort’s Residences offer additional space and privacy, with multiple bedrooms, private pools, and personalised butler service.

Gourmet Dining

Vakkaru Maldives offers a culinary journey through five unique dining venues, each providing a different experience. Amaany serves a blend of international and local flavours in a stylish, open-air setting, while Onu offers authentic Southeast Asian cuisine in a beautiful bamboo-enclosed pavilion. For a more casual dining experience, guests can enjoy Mediterranean-inspired dishes at Isoletta, or fresh seafood at Vakku, the resort’s signature grill. The resort also offers private dining experiences, including a romantic dinner on the beach or a sunset cruise with a gourmet meal.

Wellness and Recreation

The Merana Spa at Vakkaru Maldives offers a range of treatments that combine traditional Maldivian healing practices with modern wellness techniques. The spa features overwater treatment rooms, a relaxation area, and a yoga pavilion, providing a peaceful setting for relaxation and rejuvenation. The resort also offers a variety of recreational activities, including diving, snorkelling, and water sports, as well as personalised excursions to explore the surrounding coral reefs and nearby islands.

Tailored Experiences

Vakkaru Maldives is dedicated to creating personalised experiences for every guest. Whether it’s a private yoga session, a customised wellness retreat, or a romantic dinner under the stars, the resort’s team is committed to making every moment special. The resort’s focus on timeless beauty and unspoiled nature ensures that every experience is not only luxurious but also deeply connected to the natural environment.

In summary

Vakkaru Maldives offers a luxurious and romantic retreat where guests can enjoy the beauty of the Maldives in a serene and elegant setting. With its elegant accommodations, exceptional dining, and personalised service, this resort provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable holiday in the Maldives.
