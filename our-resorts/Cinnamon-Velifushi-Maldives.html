Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives: Discover Modern Elegance

Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives is a chic, contemporary island resort located in Vaavu Atoll, where modern luxury meets the serene beauty of the Maldives. With its stylish villas, gourmet dining options, and tranquil atmosphere, Cinnamon Velifushi offers a sophisticated escape for discerning travellers. Whether you’re seeking a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, this resort provides an idyllic setting for relaxation and indulgence.

Stylish Villas with Modern Comforts

The accommodations at Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives are designed with a focus on modern elegance and comfort. Guests can choose from a variety of villas, including Beach Villas that offer direct access to the pristine sands, or the sleek Water Bungalows and Water Suites, which are perched above the lagoon and feature private decks with stunning ocean views. Each villa is tastefully decorated with contemporary furnishings and equipped with state-of-the-art amenities, ensuring a luxurious and comfortable stay.

Gourmet Dining in a Chic Setting

Dining at Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives is a gourmet experience, with a variety of restaurants offering both local and international cuisine. Vah, the resort’s main restaurant, serves a buffet of global dishes with live cooking stations, while Marlin, the overwater seafood restaurant, specialises in fresh catches prepared with a modern twist. For a more casual dining experience, Hama Rhythmic Bar offers light snacks and refreshing drinks in a vibrant, music-filled setting. The resort also offers private dining experiences, allowing guests to enjoy a romantic meal on the beach or in the privacy of their villa.

Unwind and Rejuvenate at Mandara Spa

The Mandara Spa at Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives offers a range of treatments designed to promote relaxation and well-being. The spa’s tranquil setting, with treatment rooms overlooking the lagoon, provides the perfect backdrop for a variety of wellness therapies, including Balinese massages, facials, and body scrubs. Guests can also enjoy yoga sessions on the beach, meditation classes, and personalised wellness programs, all designed to rejuvenate the body and mind.

Activities for Every Interest

Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives offers a wide range of activities for guests to enjoy. The resort’s Dive Center provides access to some of the Maldives’ best dive sites, where guests can explore vibrant coral reefs and encounter a variety of marine life. For those who prefer to stay above the water, the resort offers snorkelling, windsurfing, and kayaking, as well as fishing trips and sunset cruises. On land, guests can relax by the infinity pool, participate in beach games, or explore the island’s natural beauty on a guided nature walk.

Why Choose Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives?

Cinnamon Velifushi Maldives offers a chic and contemporary escape in the heart of the Maldives. With its stylish villas, gourmet dining, and tranquil atmosphere, the resort provides a perfect blend of modern luxury and natural beauty. Whether you’re looking to unwind, explore, or simply indulge, Cinnamon Velifushi offers an unforgettable island experience for all types of travellers.
