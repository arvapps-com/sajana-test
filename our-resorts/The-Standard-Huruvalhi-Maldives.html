Playful Luxury at The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives

The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives, located in the Raa Atoll, redefines the traditional Maldivian resort experience with its chic, vibrant, and playful approach to luxury. Ideal for millennials, couples, and groups of friends, this boutique resort offers a lively atmosphere where fun and relaxation go hand in hand. From stylish overwater villas to lively dining venues, The Standard promises an unforgettable island escape that’s anything but ordinary.

Stylish Overwater and Beachfront Villas

The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives, offers a range of accommodations designed to reflect its playful and modern aesthetic. The overwater villas feature bright, colourful interiors and expansive decks with private plunge pools, hammocks, and direct lagoon access. Beachfront villas offer private gardens, outdoor showers, and easy access to the island’s white sandy beaches. Each villa is equipped with modern amenities, including rain showers, plush bedding, and floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the stunning ocean views.

Lively Dining and Social Experiences

Dining at The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives, is a vibrant experience, with multiple venues offering a mix of global flavours and local specialties. The resort’s main restaurant, Kula, serves a buffet of international dishes with live cooking stations. For something unique, Guduguda offers traditional Maldivian cuisine in a communal, family-style setting. The resort also features a barbecue grill, overwater lounge, and poolside bar where guests can enjoy cocktails, light bites, and live music. The Standard is known for its lively social scene, with regular events, DJ sets, and themed parties that create a fun and energetic atmosphere.

Unique Experiences and Activities

The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives, offers a variety of unique experiences designed to entertain and delight guests. From underwater snorkelling trails to guided night dives, the resort’s Dive Center provides opportunities to explore the vibrant marine life of the Maldives. On land, guests can participate in creative workshops, mixology classes, and wellness programs. The resort’s spa, The Standard Spa, offers a playful twist on traditional treatments, with a focus on fun and relaxation. The spa features a communal hammam, relaxation lounge, and open-air yoga pavilion.

Wellness with a Twist at The Standard Spa

The Standard Spa offers a unique approach to wellness, combining relaxation with a touch of fun. The spa’s menu includes massages, facials, and body treatments that use natural and organic products. Guests can also enjoy group wellness activities, such as sunrise yoga, sound healing sessions, and guided meditation. The spa’s vibrant and colourful design, combined with its playful approach to wellness, ensures that every visit is both rejuvenating and enjoyable.

Why Choose The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives?

The Standard, Huruvalhi Maldives, is the perfect choice for travellers seeking a fun, vibrant, and stylish island getaway. With its chic overwater villas, lively dining venues, and unique experiences, the resort offers a refreshing and modern take on the traditional Maldivian escape.
