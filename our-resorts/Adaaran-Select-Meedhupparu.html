Experience the All-Inclusive Luxury of Adaaran Select Meedhupparu

Adaaran Select Meedhupparu, located in Raa Atoll, offers an exceptional all-inclusive experience in the Maldives. This expansive resort is perfect for families, couples, and groups, with a variety of accommodations, dining options, and activities designed to cater to every guest’s needs. With its stunning beaches, crystal-clear lagoon, and lush greenery, Adaaran Select Meedhupparu is the ideal destination for a luxurious yet relaxed island escape.

Luxurious Water Villas with Ocean Views

The resort features a wide range of villas, each designed to offer comfort and luxury in a beautiful tropical setting. Guests can choose from Beach Villas, set along the shoreline with direct beach access, or the spacious Water Villas, perched above the lagoon with private sundecks and stunning ocean views. For families or groups, the Family Beach Villas offer extra space and convenient access to the resort’s amenities. Each villa is tastefully decorated with modern furnishings and equipped with all the amenities needed for a comfortable stay.

Delight in Diverse Dining Experiences

Adaaran Select Meedhupparu offers a variety of dining options, each providing a unique culinary experience. Jaafaiy, the resort’s main buffet restaurant, serves a wide array of international dishes, with live cooking stations and themed nights that showcase different cuisines. Sufura offers an authentic taste of the Maldives, with traditional dishes made from fresh local ingredients. For those seeking a more intimate dining experience, the resort’s à la carte restaurants, including the overwater Thavaa and the seafood-specialty restaurant Café Mass, provide elegant settings with spectacular views.

Activities for Every Interest

The resort offers a wide range of activities to keep guests entertained throughout their stay. The Watersports Center provides equipment and lessons for windsurfing, jet-skiing, kayaking, and more. Guests can also explore the underwater world with guided snorkeling and diving excursions, or enjoy a leisurely sunset cruise around the atoll. On land, the resort features a tennis court, a fitness centre, and a spa where guests can relax and rejuvenate with a variety of treatments. For families, the resort offers a Kids Club with daily activities, ensuring that younger guests have plenty of fun.

Relaxation at Mandara Spa

The Mandara Spa at Adaaran Select Meedhupparu is a tranquil retreat where guests can unwind and indulge in a range of treatments. The spa offers massages, facials, body treatments, and wellness therapies, all designed to promote relaxation and well-being. The spa’s serene setting, with treatment rooms overlooking the lagoon, enhances the experience, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in a state of tranquillity.

Why Choose Adaaran Select Meedhupparu?

Adaaran Select Meedhupparu is the perfect choice for travellers seeking an all-inclusive resort that offers something for everyone. With its luxurious accommodations, diverse dining options, and wide range of activities, the resort provides an ideal setting for a memorable Maldivian getaway. Whether you’re travelling with family, friends, or as a couple, Adaaran Select Meedhupparu offers an unforgettable experience in one of the world’s most beautiful destinations.
