Maldives Tour Package

Duration: 03 Nights & 04 Days

Location:Adaraan Prestige Vadoo, Adaaran Club Rannalhi

Package Overview

This Maldives Honeymoon Package is designed for couples looking to celebrate their love in a stunning tropical paradise. Experience the serene beauty of the Maldives with luxury accommodations, delicious meals, and special honeymoon perks to make your stay memorable.

Package Details

Detail Description
Type of Package: Maldives Honeymoon Package
Arrival & Departure: Male International Airport [MLE]
Rooms Allotted: 01 Night Sunset Ocean Villa with Plunge Pool & Jacuzzi + 02 Nights Classic Room
Airport Transfer: Speedboat
No. of Pax: 02 Adults in 01 double room
Meal Plan: All Inclusive (All Meals + Beverages)
Package Cost: INR 170,500/-

Day-wise Itinerary

Day Details
Day 1 Arrival at Male International Airport and transfer to Adaraan Prestige Vadoo via speedboat. Check-in and relax.
Day 2 Enjoy the luxury of your Sunset Ocean Villa. Indulge in spa treatments or relax by the beach.
Day 3 Transfer to Adaaran Club Rannalhi. Explore the vibrant marine life through snorkeling or enjoy the resort's amenities.
Day 4 Check-out and transfer back to Male International Airport for your departure.
