Package Overview

Type of Package: Thailand Holiday Package

Package Details

Detail Description
Rooms Allotted:
  • 3 Nights at Centara Pattaya - Deluxe Room
  • 3 Nights at Deevana Plaza Krabi - Deluxe Room
  • 2 Nights at Sea View Patong - Superior Room
Number of Pax: 04 Adults in 02 Double Rooms
Meal Plan: Breakfast Only

Itinerary

Day Itinerary
Day 01 (23-Nov) Centara Pattaya - Deluxe Room. Pvt transfer from BKK APT to PTY HTL by van. Evening proceed to Alcazar Show (Std Ticket).
Day 02 (24-Nov) Centara Pattaya - Deluxe Room. Breakfast at hotel. Coral Island Tour + Speed Boat + Lunch + SIC.
Day 03 (25-Nov) Ramada DMA Bangkok - Superior Room. Breakfast at hotel. Pvt transfer from PTY HTL to BKK HTL by van.
Day 04 (26-Nov) Ramada DMA Bangkok - Superior Room. Breakfast at hotel. Proceed to BKK City Tour + Golden Temple + Mini Recycling + Gems Gallery + SIC.
Day 05 (27-Nov) Deevana Plaza Krabi - Deluxe Room. Breakfast at hotel. Pvt transfer from BKK HTL to BKK APT by van. Pvt transfer from HKT APT to Krabi HTL by van. Day for leisure.
Day 06 (28-Nov) Deevana Plaza Krabi - Deluxe Room. Breakfast at hotel. Proceed to Krabi 4 Island Tour + Long Tail Boat + Picnic Lunch + SIC.
Day 07 (29-Nov) Sea View Patong - Superior Room. Breakfast at hotel. Pvt transfer from Krabi HTL to HKT HTL by van. Day for leisure.
Day 08 (30-Nov) Sea View Patong - Superior Room. Breakfast at hotel. Proceed to Phi Phi Island Tour + Big Boat + Lunch + SIC + Ex National Park Fee.
Day 09 (1-Dec) Last Day Trip Hotel. Breakfast at hotel. Pvt transfer from HKT HTL to HKT APT by van.

Booking Process

  • First, our reservations will send you a final confirmation email with the complete package details.
  • For confirming the package, please send us your passport copies (of both persons traveling) & PAN card copy (of 01 person) as a reply to the final confirmation email sent by the reservations. Also, mention your traveling date in the email.
  • After the details are received from your end, our reservations will book the complete package in your name.
  • After the official confirmation from the hotel, our reservations will share the booking confirmation email, which includes the complete package details, the booking confirmation codes & the payment details.
  • Once the confirmation is received, a token advance of 10% of the package cost is required.
  • 100% payment must be cleared before the payment cut-off date provided by the reservations. (Before making payment, you can also cross-check your bookings directly at hotels)
