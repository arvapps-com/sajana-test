Coorg & Bandipur Tour Package

Duration of tour: 03 Nights & 04 Days

Pick up & Drop: Bangalore

Stay Period: May 2024

Detailed Itinerary

Day Details
Day 01 Bangalore to Coorg. Pick up from Bangalore and visit Golden Temple on the way.
Day 2 Coorg sightseeing. Visit Abbey Falls, Raj's Seat, Dubare Elephant Camp, and Nasargadhama activity park. Overnight stay at the Hotel/Resort.
Day 3 Jeep Safari day at Bandipur. (Need to be there by 2 PM)
Day 4 Bandipur to Bangalore. After breakfast, check out & drop at Bangalore.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions Exclusions
Accommodation as specified above using 01 dbl rooms. Any meals other than those mentioned above.
Transfers and sightseeing using AC Sedan. Any Airfare / Train fare.
Welcome drink on arrival. Entry fees/ any boating charges.
Complimentary breakfast in all hotels. Any portage at airports and hotels, tips, insurance, wine, mineral water, telephone charges, and all items of personal nature.
Service of well-experienced English-speaking tour driver. Any services not specifically mentioned in the inclusions.
Vehicle at your disposal for arrival and departure transfers & sightseeing as per itinerary. Expenses caused by factors beyond our control like rail and flight delays, roadblocks, and vehicle malfunctions.
Fuel, driver allowance, toll, parking fee, and night halt charges. Child above mentioned age limit/max permitted number will be charged extra.
All applicable taxes.

Special Note

  • Confirmation of rooms is subject to availability at the time of booking. In case the hotel mentioned cannot confirm standard rooms, alternative accommodation shall be provided and any extra cost will be paid in addition to the charges mentioned above.
  • Rate is valid for the above-mentioned travel date only.
  • Mandatory RT-PCR test to be done at the resort.

Hotel Details

Day 01 - 02: Coorg Gokulam Grand (Standard Honeycomb)

Day 03: Bandipur MC Resort (Deluxe Room Non A/C)

Booking Process

  • Our reservations will send you a final confirmation mail with the complete package details.
  • To confirm the package, please send us your passport copies (of both persons traveling) & PAN card copy (of 01 person) as a reply to the final confirmation mail sent by reservations, also mention your traveling date in the mail.
  • After receiving the details, our reservations will book the complete package in your name.
  • After the official confirmation from the hotel, our reservations will share the booking confirmation mail, which includes the complete package details, booking confirmation codes & payment details.
  • Once confirmation is received, a token advance of 10% of the package cost is required.
  • 100% payment must be cleared before the payment cut-off date provided by reservations. (You can cross-check your bookings directly at the hotels before making payment).
