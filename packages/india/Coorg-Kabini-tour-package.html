Coorg & Kabini Tour Package

Duration of tour: 03 Nights & 04 Days

Pick up & Drop: Bangalore

Detailed Itinerary

Day Details
Day 01 Bangalore to Coorg. Pick up from Bangalore and visit Golden Temple on the way.
Day 02 Coorg sightseeing. Visit Abbey Falls, Raj’s Seat, Dubare Elephant Camp, Nasargadhama activity park, and enjoy boating. Overnight stay at the Hotel/Resort.
Day 03 Coorg to Kabini. After breakfast, check out and drive to Kabini. Explore Kabini River, Kabini Dam, Nagarhole National Park, Irpu Falls, Rameswara Temple, etc. Overnight stay at the Hotel/Resort.
Day 04 Kabini to Bangalore. After breakfast, check out & drop at Bangalore.

Package Inclusions & Exclusions

Inclusions Exclusions
Accommodation in specified hotels using 01 dbl room. Any meals other than those mentioned above.
Transfers and sightseeing using AC Sedan. Any Airfare / Train fare.
Welcome drink on arrival. Entry fees/ any boating charges.
Complimentary breakfast at the hotel. Any portage at airports and hotels, tips, insurance, wine, mineral water, telephone charges, etc.
Service of an experienced English-speaking driver. Any services not specifically mentioned in the inclusions.
Vehicle for arrival, departure transfers, & sightseeing as per itinerary. Expenses caused by factors beyond control like delays, roadblocks, etc.
Fuel, driver allowance, toll, parking fees, and night halt charges. Child above specified age limit/max permitted number will incur extra charges.
All applicable taxes.

Special Note

  • Confirmation of rooms is subject to availability at the time of booking. Alternative accommodation will be provided if needed, and any extra cost will be borne by the traveler.
  • Rate is valid for the specified travel dates only.
  • Mandatory RT-PCR test at the resort.

Hotel Details

Day 01 - 02: Coorg Gokulam Grand (Standard Honeycomb)

Day 03: Kabini Regenta (Courtyard)

Booking Process

  • Our reservations will send you a final confirmation mail with the complete package details.
  • To confirm the package, please send a copy of your Aadhaar in reply to the final confirmation mail, along with your travel dates.
  • Our reservations team will then book the package in your name.
  • Once the hotel confirms, we will send a booking confirmation mail with details, codes, and payment instructions.
  • A token advance of 10% of the package cost is required after confirmation.
  • 100% payment must be cleared before the provided cut-off date. You can cross-check bookings directly with hotels before making the final payment.
