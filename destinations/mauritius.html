Practical Travel Tips for Mauritius

Best Time to Visit: Mauritius is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is during the dry season, from May to December, when the weather is cool and pleasant for beach activities and sightseeing.

Visa Requirements: Indian citizens visiting Mauritius for tourism do not require a visa for stays of up to 90 days. Ensure your passport is valid for at least six months from the date of arrival.

Language and Communication: English is the official language, but French and Creole are widely spoken. Learning a few phrases in French or Creole can make your trip more enjoyable.

Currency: The currency in Mauritius is the Mauritian Rupee (MUR). Credit cards are widely accepted, but itâ€™s advisable to carry cash for small purchases in local markets and rural areas.

Safety: Mauritius is generally safe for travellers, but itâ€™s always a good idea to take standard precautions, such as not leaving valuables unattended on the beach.
